Our History

Rafting Adventures was founded in 1995 by a team of passionate outdoor explorers who believed that the thrill of the river should be shared with the world. Over the years, we've grown from a small operation into a world-class rafting company.

From the challenging rapids of Los Dientes del Diablo to the serene beauty of our Sunset Rafting trips, our journey is rooted in delivering unforgettable experiences to adventurers around the world.
